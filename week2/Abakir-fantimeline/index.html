Brandon Beck is a co-founder of Riot games, a company that has been working in game making. Riot’s most popular game is League of Legend which has not only made Riot a prominent company in the gaming world but also given identity to Brandon Beck in the gaming world.

2006: Riot Games was founded by Brandon Beck and Marc Merrill
He established gaming company named Riot with the aim of bringing “Gamer’s Games” to players around the world. Along with co-founder, Beck started to work on creating high-quality online games with the innovative approach.
2009: Riot Games released League of Legends and started to beome well known
